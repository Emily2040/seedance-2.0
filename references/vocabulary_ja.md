# Appendix E: Japanese Cinematic & Photography Vocabulary

This reference provides standardized Japanese terminology for precise communication with the Seedance 2.0 model, which often responds well to native technical terms.

---

## E.1 景別 (Shot Sizes)

| Term (JP) | Reading | English Equivalent | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **超ロングショット** | Cho Rongu Shotto | Extreme Long Shot (ELS) | Focus on environment/scale |
| **ロングショット** | Rongu Shotto | Long Shot (LS) | Full body + environment |
| **フルショット** | Furu Shotto | Full Shot (FS) | Head to toe |
| **ニーショット** | Ni Shotto | Knee Shot | From knees up |
| **ウエストショット** | Uesuto Shotto | Waist Shot | From waist up |
| **バストショット** | Basuto Shotto | Bust Shot | From chest up |
| **クローズアップ** | Kurozu Appu | Close-Up (CU) | Face focus |
| **特写 / 大写し** | Tokusha / O-utsushi | Extreme Close-Up (ECU) | Detail focus (eyes, hands) |

---

## E.2 運鏡 (Camera Movements)

| Term (JP) | Reading | English Equivalent | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **パン** | Pan | Pan (Horizontal) | Side-to-side sweep |
| **ティルト** | Tiruto | Tilt (Vertical) | Up-and-down pivot |
| **ドリー** | Dori | Dolly | Moving camera on tracks |
| **ズーム** | Zumu | Zoom | Changing focal length |
| **トラック** | Torakku | Trucking | Moving sideways with subject |
| **クレーンショット** | Kuren Shotto | Crane Shot | High-elevation movement |
| **手持ち / ハンドヘルド** | Temochi / Handoherudo | Handheld | Organic, shaky motion |
| **フィックス** | Fikkusu | Static / Fixed Shot | No camera movement |

| Term (JP) | Reading | English Equivalent | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **三分割法** | San-bunkatsu-ho | Rule of Thirds | Standard grid composition |
| **日の丸構図** | Hinomaru-kozu | Center Composition | Subject in dead center |

| **シンメトリー** | Shinmetori | Symmetry | Balanced, mirrored framing |

## E.4 灯光術語 (Lighting Terminology)

| Term (JP) | Reading | English Equivalent | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **順光** | Junko | Front Lighting | Light from camera direction |
| **逆光** | Gyakko | Backlighting | Light from behind subject |
| **サイド光** | Saido-ko | Side Lighting | High contrast, dramatic |
| **半逆光** | Han-gyakko | Rim Lighting | Highlights edges |
| **柔らかい光** | Yawarakai Hikari | Soft Light | Diffused, low shadow |
| **硬い光** | Katai Hikari | Hard Light | Sharp, defined shadows |

## E.6 Direct-use Japanese Phrases

- **「実写映画のような質感」** (Texture like a live-action movie)
- **「被写界深度を浅く」** (Shallow depth of field)
- **「逆光でシルエットを強調」** (Emphasize silhouette with backlighting)
- **「スローモーションで劇的に」** (Dramatically in slow motion)
- **「手持ちカメラの臨場感」** (Realism of a handheld camera)
